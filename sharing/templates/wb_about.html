WAYbetter offers a new, cost-effective transportation alternative. Door to door, personalized, efficient and enviormental taxi service.

WAYbetter offers two different taxi services:

  1. A private taxi ride for a fixed, pre determined price.
  2. A WAYbetter taxi ride which maximizes the efficiency of the taxi in terms of occupancy, meaning you pay less for the ride while still enjoying great taxi service.

Both services offer a great advantage of online booking and payment.

Remember - WAYbetter rides are cheaper thanks to sharing between different passengers. Therefore, the more passengers use the system the better it gets, price decreases and service level increases.
